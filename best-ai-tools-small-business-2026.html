Frequently Asked Questions

What AI tools are free for small business?

ChatGPT Free, Canva Free, Grammarly Free, Copy.ai Free, and Notion Free all offer genuinely useful free tiers. You can build a basic AI stack at zero cost.

How much should a small business spend on AI tools?

We recommend starting with $20-50/month (ChatGPT Plus + one other tool) and scaling up based on ROI. If a tool saves you 5+ hours per month, it's paying for itself many times over.

Will AI replace my employees?

For most small businesses, AI augments your team rather than replacing it. It handles repetitive tasks (drafting, scheduling, basic design) so your people can focus on strategy, relationships, and creative work that AI can't do as well.
