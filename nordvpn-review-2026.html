NordVPN's security credentials are among the strongest in the industry:

NordVPN had a server breach in 2019. Since then, they've completely overhauled their infrastructure: all servers are now RAM-only, they've commissioned multiple independent audits, and they launched a bug bounty program. In our assessment, NordVPN has handled the aftermath responsibly and is now more transparent about security than most competitors.
